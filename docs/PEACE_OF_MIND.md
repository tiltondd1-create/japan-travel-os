## Content Rules

- Use `Likely`, `Ask first`, or `Avoid` for GF confidence.
- Add a source/contact date for ryokan and restaurant confirmations.
- Reconfirm before the trip because menus and ingredients change.
- Avoid claiming “safe” unless the venue has directly confirmed the relevant requirement.
- Store only the medical information you are comfortable keeping in the workbook.
